BIIT

Be Involved In Tech. A community platform helping young Africans break into tech not through textbooks, but through real projects, real mentors, and real experience.

The gap between "wanting to learn tech" and "building a career in tech" is enormous in Africa. BIIT bridges that gap by connecting young people with a community that builds together — turning beginners into contributors through hands-on experience.

Hands-on project tracks in web, mobile, and data
Mentorship from working professionals in tech
Portfolio building through real-world projects
Peer learning circles and accountability groups
Open source contribution pathways
Fully free access for young learners
