How It Started

Born from a gap in the ecosystem.

Brivent was founded in Lagos in 2024 by a group of builders who kept running into the same problem: great ideas in Africa were dying not from lack of talent, but from lack of infrastructure.

The tools were fragmented. The networks were closed. The support systems that founders in Silicon Valley took for granted simply didn't exist at scale on the continent. So we decided to build them.

We started by building products that solved our own problems — automation tools, community platforms, education systems. And as those products grew, so did our vision: not just to build for Africa, but to build the infrastructure that makes Africa build better.

What Drives Us

Our core values.

Four principles that guide every decision we make, every product we ship, and every community we build.

Clarity

We believe that clear thinking leads to better building. We define problems precisely before we build solutions, and we communicate with radical transparency.

Integrity

We do what we say we'll do. Trust is the foundation of every product we ship and every partnership we build. We don't cut corners when it comes to our word.

Trust

We earn trust through consistency, competence, and care. Every user who touches a Brivent product should feel supported, understood, and valued.

Growth

We're obsessed with growth — not just of our business, but of the people we work with and the communities we build. Rising tides lift all boats.
