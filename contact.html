FAQ

Common questions.

What kind of projects does Brivent take on?

We focus on digital products, community platforms, and startup infrastructure. We're especially interested in projects that serve African markets or have a clear social impact alongside commercial viability.

How do I get early access to WhatsAuto, Kinnoc, or BIIT?

Fill out the contact form above, select the relevant product inquiry, and tell us a bit about your use case. We'll get back to you within 48 hours with next steps.

Is Brivent open to investment or partnership?

Yes — we're selectively open to partnerships and investment conversations that align with our mission. Reach out via the form with "Partnership Opportunity" selected and tell us more about your interest.

Can I join the Brivent team?

We're always interested in meeting exceptional builders, designers, and community architects. Send us your story and what you'd want to work on — we'll keep it on file and reach out when the right opportunity opens.
